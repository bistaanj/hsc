Operations

Our team of industry professionals brings a broad spectrum of expertise to support mining companies across multiple facets of their operations. We understand that mining companies require specialized insights to navigate the complexities of the industry, and we are equipped to provide tailored solutions in key areas:
  • Machinery & Equipment: We assist in assessing the value, functionality, and efficiency of mining machinery and equipment, ensuring our clients have the right tools in place to meet production goals and maintain operational excellence.
  • Offtake Agreements: Our experts provide guidance in structuring and valuing offtake agreements, helping mining companies secure reliable markets for their products and establish mutually beneficial terms with buyers.
  • Farm-in / Farm-out: We offer valuation and advisory services on farm-in and farm-out arrangements, enabling clients to attract strategic partners, optimize resource allocation, and expand or divest their asset portfolios effectively.
  • Annual Reporting: Our team aids in preparing annual reports by providing accurate and comprehensive asset valuations, ensuring compliance with financial reporting standards and helping stakeholders understand the full value of the company's assets.
  • Compliance: We support mining companies in meeting regulatory and industry compliance requirements, delivering valuations that align with relevant standards to promote transparency and mitigate risk.
With our extensive knowledge and experience, we empower mining companies to make informed decisions and enhance operational efficiency across every aspect of their business.

Mining Consulting

We specialize in the valuation of mineral exploration and development assets, operational mines, and oil & gas operations, catering to the unique needs of each stage in the resource lifecycle. Our comprehensive valuations support critical activities, ensuring our clients are well-prepared to navigate the complex landscape of the mining and energy sectors. Key valuation purposes include:
  • Fundraising: For companies seeking capital, we provide accurate and robust valuations that reflect the true potential of their assets, supporting effective investor communication and helping to secure funding on favorable terms.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: In M&A transactions, we deliver thorough valuations of mineral and energy assets to facilitate informed decision-making, fair negotiations, and successful deal structuring.
  • Listing Rule Compliance: We help companies meet regulatory requirements for listing, offering valuation services that align with stock exchange standards and ensure compliance, transparency, and investor confidence.
  • Offtake Agreements: Our valuations assist clients in structuring and negotiating offtake agreements, providing clarity on asset value to establish strong, sustainable partnerships with buyers.
  • Machinery & Equipment: We offer specialized machinery and equipment valuations, assessing asset condition and productivity potential, which are essential for optimizing operational efficiency and justifying capital expenditures.
Our expertise ensures that each valuation is tailored to meet the strategic goals and compliance needs of our clients, helping them make well-informed decisions and positioning them for growth and success in the resource sector.

Compliance

Our team has extensive experience providing advanced professional services to companies listed on major international stock exchanges, including HKEx, SGX, ASX, LSE, TSX, and JSE. With a deep understanding of global regulatory environments, we specialize in preparing public disclosure reports that meet stringent compliance requirements across various jurisdictions. Our compliance services include:
  • JORC / NI 43-101 / SAMREC Reports: We prepare technical reports that comply with globally recognized standards such as JORC, NI 43-101, and SAMREC, ensuring that mineral resource and reserve data are accurately and transparently disclosed.
  • PRMS / NI 51-101 Reports: For oil and gas sector compliance, we generate PRMS (Petroleum Resources Management System) and NI 51-101 reports, delivering the detailed and standardized evaluations essential for industry compliance and investor confidence.
  • VALMIN / CIMVAL / SAMVAL Compliant Valuations: Our valuations align with standards like VALMIN, CIMVAL, and SAMVAL, providing consistency and accuracy in assessing mineral and exploration assets according to region-specific requirements.
  • IFRS & IVS Valuations: We conduct valuations in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and International Valuation Standards (IVS), helping clients ensure their financial disclosures are reliable and compliant with global accounting norms.
Our expertise in regulatory standards allows us to support clients in meeting compliance mandates, reducing regulatory risks, and enhancing transparency for stakeholders across the globe.
